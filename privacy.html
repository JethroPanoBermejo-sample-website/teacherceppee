Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Teacher Ceppee Review Center (TCRC) ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while you browse our website and access our free educational resources. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, view our educational videos, and access free mock board exams for LET (Licensure Examination for Teachers) preparation.

2. About Our Website

Our website serves as an informational platform that provides:

  • Free educational videos for LET exam preparation
  • Free mock board examination materials
  • Information about our coaching services and programs
  • Success stories from our students
  • Contact information to inquire about premium coaching services

Note: This website does not require user registration or account creation. To enroll in our premium coaching programs, students contact us directly through our Facebook page.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Website Usage Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and general location information
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Referring website (if you came from another site)
  • Date and time of your visit

3.2 Free Resource Usage

  • Educational videos watched and viewing duration
  • Mock board exams accessed or downloaded
  • Interaction with free educational materials

3.3 Contact Information

We do not collect personal information directly through this website. For enrollment inquiries, students contact us through our Facebook page where Facebook's privacy policies apply.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Improving our website functionality and user experience
  • Understanding which educational resources are most helpful
  • Monitoring website performance and security
  • Analyzing traffic patterns to optimize content delivery
  • Ensuring our free resources are accessible and useful
  • Technical maintenance and troubleshooting

5. Free Educational Resources

Our website provides free access to educational materials including:

  • Educational Videos: Free sample lessons and teaching content accessible to all visitors
  • Mock Board Exams: Practice tests available for download and use
  • Study Materials: Publicly available educational documents and resources

Privacy Note: When you access these free resources, we may track usage anonymously to improve content quality, but we do not link this data to your personal identity.

6. Facebook Communication for Enrollment

To enroll in our premium coaching services, students contact us through our Facebook page:

  • Communication through Facebook Messenger is governed by Facebook's Privacy Policy
  • We only receive information you choose to share when messaging us
  • Enrollment discussions and arrangements are handled privately through Facebook
  • We do not store Facebook conversation data on our website systems

Important: Please review Facebook's Privacy Policy for information about how Meta handles your communications on their platform.

7. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent any information. Limited sharing may occur only for:

  • Website hosting and technical service providers
  • Analytics services to understand website usage (anonymized data only)
  • Legal compliance when required by law
  • Website security and fraud prevention

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information collected through our website:

  • Secure hosting and SSL encryption for website traffic
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited data collection to minimize privacy risks
  • Protection against unauthorized access and data breaches

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience:

  • Essential cookies: For basic website functionality
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors use our site
  • Performance cookies: To optimize loading times and user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

10. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services:

  • Video hosting: For educational content delivery
  • Web analytics: To understand website usage patterns
  • CDN services: For faster content delivery
  • Facebook: For enrollment communications (subject to Facebook's privacy policy)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Regarding information we collect:

  • You can browse our website without providing personal information
  • You can control cookies through your browser settings
  • You can contact us to request information about data we may have collected
  • You can request deletion of any data associated with your visits

Note: Since we don't require accounts or store personal information directly, most data collection is minimal and anonymous.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
